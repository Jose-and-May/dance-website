WOW! What a weekend! Yes, I know is Tuesday but this weekend was so intense that we need full Monday to recover and process all the fantastic things have happened. Salsa Solo Competition and Brisbane Latin Festival – Food, Wine, Music & Dance were amazing! Thank you Carlos and Elli for the perfect organisation, and for let us be part of the event. Can’t wait for next year…The Mambo King will be here!!! Meanwhile relax back with some Tango from Saturday night.
