The story of the project

 

We are Jose & May (Argentina & Spain and now proudly Aussie representatives). Our project is to represent Australia at the World Latin Dance Cup in Miami in December 2015 and we need your help. We are the current Australian Tango Professional Champions 2015 and we won the title with this performance at the Australia Latin Dance Championship in Sydney.

 

 

We have been granted the Distinguished Talent visa by the Australian Government in 2014 for our Tango and Latin Dance knowledge. This has given us the opportunity to live in Australia and be able to complete our AUSTRALIAN DREAM. We moved to Australia 5 years ago and we have been achieving different awards and recognitions in Australia and around the world in this time.

 

28 years of dance experience between both of us have driven us to this opportunity. Now it’s time to show to the world what we can do BUT… we want the biggest prize to complete this dance career, we want to compete at the WORLD LATIN DANCE CUP in Miami in December … and we need your help to get there…

 

 

If you want to give us a help on Jose & May Dance.
