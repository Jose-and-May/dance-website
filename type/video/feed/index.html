The Australian Salsa Open (ASO)

The Australian Salsa Open (ASO) is a national competition and is the official and only entry to World Salsa Open in Puerto Rico for Australian and New Zealand dancers. Since 2012, the ASO has also featured a Latin category, making it the first competition in Australia to offer competitors the opportunity to compete for an overall National Championship in the Latin styles of Bachata, Cha Cha and Zouk (for couples) or Samba (for solo).

The ASO is affiliated with the Annual Puerto Rico Salsa Congress and World Salsa Open and as such is the only competition to offer Australian representative status to the World Salsa Open.
