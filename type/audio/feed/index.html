National Tango champions live in Brisbane, now aiming for Worlds

I wonder whether Dancing with the Stars and Strictly Ballroom – at QPAC from today – are giving people the dancing bug.

Left: Jose and May. Image by Loretta Ryan/612 ABC Brisbane. I wonder whether Dancing with the Stars and Strictly Ballroom – at QPAC from today – are giving people the dancing bug…

They are husband and wife team Jose Camean and May Carreras – from Argentina and Spain. And now that they’re Australian champions, they’re hoping to complete their Australian Dream by going one step further and winning the World titles in the United States.Did you know that the current Australian Tango champions live in Brisbane and they teach at Woolloongabba?
